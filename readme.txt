== Description ==

Boostmyproducts allows you to easily manage your products or services from a spreadsheet. All your products or services will be visible on your site and on google from a spreadsheet.
You only have to modify the spreadsheet on Google Docs.
The contents of this file can be managed manually or synchronized with a base type of external CRM or ERP products.
Automatically, your site is updated and your visitors can use a user-friendly search system. 
In the hour following your changes, a product page is produced on wordpress and will be referenced by google in order to easily find your products.
Your products are always up-to-date.

== Installation ==


1. Fill out the form here : https://docs.google.com/a/solution-internet.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeDro0uhmjsisAi9zC4l6eeuZQZ9ZlEE8sP9F1acUxnreYMWQ/viewform
2. You will receive an email with your credentials
3. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/silic_boostmyproducts` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
4. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
5. Use the Settings->Boostmyproducts screen to configure the plugin. Set the url of webservice, your client code and your identification key.
6. Call the url http://www.mysite.com/?silic-plugin=boostmyproducts-execute in order to generate the pages (replace "www.mysite.com" with your domain name).
